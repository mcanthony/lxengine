About

Athile Technologies is a small software development consulting company aiming to develop custom solutions for independent designers, design firms, and individuals for their professional as well as personal projects.

We seek out small projects to develop visualization and presentation elements for web sites using the latest in HTML technologies, for mobile platforms such as the iPad and Android, and for desktop applications that utilitize the full power of a hosted GPU.

In More Detail...

Athile Technologies is a small, independent consulting company aiming to develop software for designers in the form of task-tailored tools and visualization technologies.

We are eager to work on the web, on mobile applications, and on the desktop. Our engineers have experience ranging from low-level C++ written for the GPU to high-level web stylization technologies such as LESS. The Athile Technologies engineering is composed exclusively of programmers who are interested in design: they know there is both form and function in all aspects of a project. It is a team that understands the importance of theory and the necessity of practice; and thrives on defining the correct balance between the two. We will produce only the highest-quality deliverables including well-written documentation, a usable and intuitive API, and (of course) cleanly written and standards-oriented code.

Athile Technologies looks forward to working with clients who want high-quality deliverables that uniquely serve their requirements rather than off-the-shelf generic solutions or poorly re-themed approximations of what is truly desired.
