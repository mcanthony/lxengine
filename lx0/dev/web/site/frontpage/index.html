About

Athile Technologies is an small software development consulting company aiming to develop custom solutions for independent designers, design firms, and individuals for their professional and personal projects.

We seek out small projects to develop custom visualization and presentation elements for web sites using the latest in HTML technologies, mobile platforms such as the iPad and Android, and desktop applications utilitizing the power of a hosted GPU.

Athile Technologies is a small, independent consulting company aiming to develop software for designers in the form of tools and visualization technologies.

We are eager to work on the web, on mobile applications, and on the desktop. Our engineers have experience with low-level C++ written for the GPU as well as high-level web technologies such as LESS. The Athile Technologies engineering is composed exclusively of programmers who are interested in design: they know there is both form and function in all aspects of a project. It is a team that understands the importance of theory and the necessity of practice; and thrives on defining the correct balance between the two. We will produce only the highest-quality deliverables including well-written documentation, a usable and intuitive API, and (of course) cleanly written and standards-oriented code.

Athile Technologies looks forward to working with clients who want high-quality deliverables that uniquely serve their requirements rather than off-the-shelf generic solutions or poorly re-themed approximations of what is truly desired.
